Services

Let us help you grow your business.

DJ Services

We curate the playlists based on the vibe of your establishment, and our proprietary algorithm mixes the music like a DJ!

Advertisements

We support video! If your establishment has televisions, we can insert targetted advertisements on those screens, specifically designed to promote your products and businesses, and drive sales.

branding & identity

We help you develop a brand, an online presence, and a user interface easily navigated by your employees. Using our solution, you can attract more customers, provide them with high quality entertainment, and save money!

profit sharing

You provide the viewers, we provide the ads. We can quantify the number of eyes watching the ads, and profit share the ad revenue with you, the business-owner.

24/7 Support

Our support team can address any concerns, feedback, or questions relating to the Muze Music-Mixing Software, or any website/brand management.

mobile app

Smooth, DJ-like, musical transitions at the tipe of your fingers.

song-to-song nonstop transitions

No pauses between each song. No annoying ads. Just music.

Beats-per-minute matching

Our proprietary algorithm matches the BPM for each song. If you're dancing to some EDM, we make sure the tempo remains upbeat from song to song. If you're jamming to some R&B, Muze can crossfade from one slow jam to another. Also, we can slowly bring the tempo UP, or gradually bring it down.

Party mix

Are you having a party? Don't hire a DJ! Turn on Muze, and let use rock your party.

Perfect timing

Our secret sauce is in each song. Every song in our library receives time markers, optimizing the perfect time to transition in or out.
